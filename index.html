About Community Connect

Community Connect is a platform designed to empower residents to report and track local issues in their neighborhood. Our mission is to facilitate better communication between community members and local authorities to create better living environments.

How It Works

Step 1: Report an Issue

Submit details about the problem you've noticed in your community.

Step 2: Community Verification

Other community members can verify and add comments to your report.

Step 3: Resolution Process

Responsible authorities review and address the reported issues.

Step 4: Problem Solved

Issues are resolved and communities are improved.
